The Raw Leaderboard is purely based on the **formatting** of the message. To be counted as a **Raw Bruh**, a message must meet these strict criteria:

1.  **The Start:** The message must begin with the word `bruh` (case-insensitive).
2.  **The Space:** There must be exactly one space after the word "bruh".
3.  **The Number:** A sequence of numbers must immediately follow that space.
4.  **Trailing Text:** Anything written *after* the number (e.g., `bruh 5 lol`) is ignored but **does not** disqualify the message.

### ğŸ“ˆ Understanding the Analytics Graph
The **Analytics Graph** provides a visual breakdown of who "owns" the bruh culture in the community. 

#### 1. The Density Slider
Because some users have very few counts, showing every single person would make the pie chart unreadable. 
* **Filtering:** Use the slider to set a "Minimum Density." 
* **Logic:** If you set the slider to **0.500%**, only users who contribute more than 0.5% of the total server messages will get their own named slice.

#### 2. Who are the "Others"?
Any user who falls **below** your slider threshold is automatically bundled into the **Others** category.
* As you move the slider to the **right** (higher %), more people move into "Others" and the chart becomes simpler.
* As you move the slider to the **left** (lower %), more individual names appear.

#### 3. Interactivity
* **Hover:** Point your mouse at a slice to see the exact count of Raw Bruhs and the percentage share.
* **Click Legend:** You can click a name in the list next to the chart to temporarily hide them and see how the rest of the pie redistributes.

As the name suggests, the **Raw Board** is a "No-Questions-Asked" record.

* **No Self-Correction:** If you messed up the number sequence (e.g., jumping from 10 to 20), the Raw Board still counts it as a command as long as the format was correct.
* **No Spam Filter:** Every validly formatted message is a **+1**. Even if the message disobeys the official "Bruh Rules," no penalties are applied here.
* **The Future:** For a more "fair" and "filtered" experience that removes spam and self-replies, keep an eye on the **Valid Bruh Count** tab.
