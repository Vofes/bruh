The Raw Leaderboard is purely based on the **formatting** of the message. To be counted toward the **Command Count**, a message must meet these strict criteria:

1.  **The Start:** The message must begin with the word `bruh` (case-insensitive).
2.  **The Space:** There must be exactly one space after the word "bruh".
3.  **The Number:** A sequence of numbers must immediately follow that space.
4.  **Trailing Text:** Anything written *after* the number is ignored but does not disqualify the message.
